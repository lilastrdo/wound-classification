Wound Classification Questions

1. Skin abnormality?

2. Is the wound/ skin abnormality over the bony prominence area/ related to medical devices?

3. Is it an open wound?

4. Any bone/ tendon structure exposed?

5. Any subcutaneous fat exposed?

6. Does blister present?

7. Blood filled blister = Deep Tissue Injury (DTI).
Serous filled blister = Stage 2 PI.

8. Is the skin blanchable?

9. Light purple = DTI
Non-blanchable redness = Stage 1 PI.

10. Is the wound related to moisture?

11. Is the wound located at the perineal area?

12. Is the non-PI wound open?
